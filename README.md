      <p>I work at the intersection of <strong>energy systems</strong>, <strong>environmental modelling</strong>, <strong>econometrics</strong>, and <strong>machine learning</strong>, with a focus on how data-driven models can support <strong>energy transition</strong> and <strong>industrial decarbonisation</strong> decisions.       <p>My work combines econometric analysis, optimisation-based energy system modelling, environmental simulation, and machine learning for multivariate time-series forecasting. I have worked with economic, environmental, industrial, and spatial datasets, including applications in <strong>cement-sector flexibility</strong>, <strong>carbon-budget modelling</strong>, renewable energy economics, pollution analysis, and ecological forecasting. I focus on <strong>reproducible workflows</strong> that connect raw data, assumptions, scenarios, model behaviour, and decision-relevant outputs.</p>
